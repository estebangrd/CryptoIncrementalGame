Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026
Last updated: May 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Solo Studio ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use Blockchain Tycoon (the "Game"), a mobile incremental/idle game available on Android.

1. Summary

  • You do not need to register, log in, or provide any personal information to play.
  • Your in-game progress is stored locally on your device and is never sent to our servers (we have no servers).
  • Third-party services integrated in the Game (Google AdMob, Google Play Billing) may collect data as described below.
  • You can delete all of the Game's data by uninstalling it.

2. Information We Collect Directly

We do not collect or transmit any personal information directly.

The Game uses your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) to save your progress, including in-game currency balances, owned hardware, upgrades, settings, and language preference. This data stays on your device.

3. Information Collected by Third Parties

Google AdMob (advertising)

The Game displays ads through Google AdMob to support free distribution. AdMob may collect, on Google's behalf:

  • Google Advertising ID (Android)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • Device model, operating system, language
  • Ad interactions (impressions, clicks, completions)

Google uses this information to serve and measure ads. Google's full policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Opting out of personalized ads: on your device, go to Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

If you reside in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the Game shows a consent dialog (Google's User Messaging Platform) the first time you launch the Game. You can change your consent at any time through the Game's settings menu.

Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)

If you make an optional in-app purchase (such as "Remove Ads" or temporary boosters), the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We receive only the result of the transaction (success/failure) and the product purchased; we never receive your payment method or billing details. Google's policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

CoinGecko (price data)

The Game fetches public cryptocurrency price data from the CoinGecko public API to make the in-game market reflect real-world price movements. No personal data is sent — only a standard anonymous HTTP request originated from your device, including your IP address as required by any web request. CoinGecko's policy: https://www.coingecko.com/en/privacy.

4. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through any third-party service used in the Game, please contact us and we will coordinate with the relevant provider to remove it.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

We do not collect personal information ourselves, but you may have rights with respect to data collected by our third-party providers:

  • Access, correction, or deletion of advertising data: contact Google through https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Opt out of personalized ads: see the "Google AdMob" section above.
  • Delete all locally stored game data: uninstall the Game, or use the "Reset Game" option in the Settings menu.

California residents (CCPA): We do not "sell" personal information as defined by the CCPA.

6. Data Retention

  • Local game data: kept on your device until you uninstall the Game or use the in-game "Reset Game" option.
  • Third-party data: retained according to each provider's own policy, linked above.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date above and publish the new version at the same URL. Continued use of the Game after a change means you accept the updated policy. For material changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you within the Game.
